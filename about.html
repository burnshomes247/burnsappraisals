About

Alicia Burns

Background

12 Years in the DC Metro Market

A proud graduate of the University of Maryland College Park, Alicia began her career in retail real estate before transitioning into the appraisal field over a decade ago — launching Burns Appraisals as an independent practice serving the DC metro area.

With over a decade of hands-on experience in Virginia, Maryland, and DC, Alicia brings a depth of local market knowledge that is simply hard to match. She understands the neighborhoods, the pricing dynamics, and the nuances that determine value across every corner of the metro.

Alicia works closely with homeowners, real estate agents, attorneys, lenders, and estate administrators — providing appraisal reports that are accurate, defensible, and delivered on time.
